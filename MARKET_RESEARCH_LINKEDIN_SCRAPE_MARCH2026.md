## What This Data Tells AgentedIn

### 1. Singapore is the hottest market
28 of 100 results are Singapore-based. TikTok/ByteDance, Shopee, OKX, Augmentus all active. The agent talent ecosystem in Southeast Asia is ahead of Europe.

### 2. The "Agent as Worker" category is nascent but real
Only 2 explicit "agent as worker" results in this Google-indexed sample — but the LinkedIn direct search showed 12 UAE postings from G42/TALENTMATE that Google hasn't indexed yet. The category is too new for Google's index to reflect it accurately. **The real number is higher than this data shows.**

### 3. Workday's move is significant
Workday (enterprise HR/finance SaaS, ~$6B revenue) hiring a Product Manager for AI Agent in Dublin means they are building agent capabilities into their core product. Every Workday customer will interact with agents. That's millions of businesses.

### 4. Staffing firms are entering the space
Jobot, ClearlyAgile, MyRemoteTeam Inc, Superhumancy Talent Partners — traditional staffing and recruiting firms are now placing AI agent developers. This is the precursor to them placing agents-as-workers, not just agent developers.

### 5. Freelance evaluation is already a job category
Mindrift and MyRemoteTeam Inc both have "AI Agent Evaluation Analyst" roles — humans whose job is to evaluate AI agent outputs for quality. This is a bridge role: humans evaluating agents, leading to agents evaluating agents.

### 6. The geographic spread validates global demand
Roles in: US, Canada, UK, Ireland, Singapore, Hong Kong, Australia, Belgium, Nigeria, Pakistan, Ukraine, Poland, Myanmar. The agent economy is not concentrated in Silicon Valley.
